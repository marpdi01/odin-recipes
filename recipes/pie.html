Dirk's Pie

This is the same recipe for the pies used at our wedding.

Here is my favorite dish to bring to parties. No one can resist a homemade pie, especially not this one. Please enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Berries
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Pie Crust
  • Egg Wash

Steps

  1. Mix berries and sugar
  2. Roll out pie crust
  3. Add to pie tin
  4. Use egg wash
  5. Bake at 375 for 1 hour
