Dirk's Pizza

This is one of our favorite weekend recipes to share with the family.

This recipe comes from many generations of pizza makers in Italy. As a child we would make this every few weeks to feed our large family will plenty of left overs.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Veggie Toppings

Steps

  1. Roll out dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce
  3. Add chopped veggies
  4. Top with cheese
  5. Bake at 425 for 12 minutes
