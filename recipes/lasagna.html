Dirk's Lasagna

This is our family recipe for one of our favorite dishes, lasagna

You will be making this dish is steps, follow the directions to make this layered delight. You first prepare all the ingredients and make cook the noodles. Then, once everything is ready you will build the dish before baking it in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Olives
  • Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Chop the veggies
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Grate the cheese
  4. Build the lasagna
  5. Bake at 375 for 1 hour
